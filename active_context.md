# Active Knowledge Base

## Overarching Goal
Understand the business dynamics of a footwear (sneaker/zapatillas) company that sells primarily through distributors (Wholesales), plus Retail own stores and Online. The company has both stable product models and fashion-driven "boom-bust" products. We want to understand: overall revenue growth trajectory, key growth drivers, channel mix evolution, product lifecycle patterns, and concentration/fashion risks.

Investigate this by computing ASP at the model-level within each channel year-over-year to separate mix effects from true price changes.
